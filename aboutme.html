About:

Hello! My name is Justin Ling and I am currently a sophomore Web Design and Engineering student minoring in Computer Engineering at Santa Clara University. Being born and raised in the Bay Area, I've developed a passion for technology and programming; both front-end and back-end. Although I still have much to learn, I am eager to strengthen my programming skills in all areas and pursue new knowledge in our technologically-driven society. In addition to acquiring technical skills, I also strive to improve my interpersonal and business skills. On campus I am involved with Santa Clara Consulting Club, as well as the professional business fraternity: Alpha Kappa Psi. I believe that a balance of technical and interpersonal traits are key to success in any career and I hope to embody these characteristics in my future endeavors.
